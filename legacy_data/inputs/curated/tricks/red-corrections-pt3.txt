We see "Double" used in multiple contexts with different meanings:
- Double Legover (3) = two sequential legover dex elements?
- Double Fairy (3) = ?
- Double Blender (5) = ?
- Double Spinning Osis (5) = ?
Double is not its own set of moves (like stepping) but only signifies someone has done a double dexterity or double body spin.

Double Leg Over is a Miraging Legover. It gets two dex ADDs and one stall ADD.
Double Fairy term isn't used much, it is really a Double Illusion. Two dexes and a toe stall.
Double Blender is a Whirling Blender, so two dexes and an Osis = 5 ADDs.
Double Spinning Osis is just that, two spins to an Osis, = 5 ADDs.

Double can involve different trick families.


**Q5.3 — Inspinning vs Spinning: what's the difference?**

footbag.org lists both "Spinning" tricks (which we have) and "Inspinning" tricks:
- Inspinning Same Side Mirage (3 ADD)
- Inspinning Same Side Illusion (3 ADD)
- Inspinning Butterfly (4 ADD)
- Inspinning Paradox Mirage (4 ADD)
- Inspinning Paradox Illusion (4 ADD)

All have alt_name "front" — suggesting the spin direction is inward/forward.
Is "Inspinning" the same as "Spinning" with a direction qualifier, or a meaningfully different technique?
Should Inspinning tricks be separate entries or aliases for Spinning variants?

Traditional Spinning is setting the bag from a clipper and spinning away from the bag. So if it is set from a right Clipper, the player would spin counter clockwise.
Traditional Inspinning is setting the bag from a clipper and spinning toward the bag. So if it is set from a right Clipper, the player would spin clockwise.

Spinning and Inspinning are different and should be treated as such.



**Q5.4 — Tapping modifier: is it the same as what we call "Stepping"?**

footbag.org lists:
- Tap (3 ADD) = Tapping Mirage
- Tapping Illusion (3 ADD)
- Tapping Legover (3 ADD)

Our dictionary has Stepping Osis (in the Osis family) but "Tapping" is not explicitly in our modifier table.
Is Tapping the same technique as Stepping? Or is it a distinct body element?

Tapping is not Stepping. Tapping is its own group of moves. Tapping is essentially Atomic set to a same-side dex (dexing again with the same foot).
