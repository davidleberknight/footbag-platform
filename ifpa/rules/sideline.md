# 2-Square

Game is to 11, must win by 2 points, or until a player reaches 15 points.

Rally Scoring – every rally creates a point, whether you are serving or not.  CALL your score before serving.

Hand-serve from the back line. The serve must have some upward trajectory and be above the opponent's waist

You can contact the bag up to three times – upper-body is allowed, but it counts as a contact.  (Unless it's an accidental or forced contact)

You can stall, but you cannot "pass" (send the bag into the opponents square) with a stall.

Passes are just like 4-Square – must have some upward trajectory, and be above the opponent's knee.

Side and back lines are "in" (like net) Center line is "out"

It's okay to:

* Use body hits and stalls (Counts as one of your three contacts, unless the contact was forced or accidental. You must pass/attack with a kick)

* Go over, but not touch opponent's squares with arms, legs, etc. (You may not touch your opponent within their square)

* Go out-of-bounds

* Attack from out-of-bounds

* Attack through any lines (middle, side, back. i.e. you only have to get the bag into the opponents square – you don't have to cross the center line)

* Fake a contact

* Hide the bag from opponent's view (except on serve)

* …and it's okay to HAVE FUN!

# 4-Square

(Summary)

GOLDEN RULES
These rules may not be changed during the game:


You are OUT if
* The bag lands in your square
* You kick the bag on a line (All lines are out)
* You kick the bag out of bounds
* You step into someone else's square
* You make contact with someone while they are in their square


If there is a disagreement about what happened, a VOTE occurs
* Every player gets one vote - except the server who gets two votes
* If you leave the 4 square court during a vote you are automatically out
* Players do not have to vote
* Players may lie, blatant or otherwise
* Players may not change their vote.
* The outcome of the vote always determines who is out
* Tied votes result in a hack-off (A round between only the two players in question)
* TOO MUCH VOTING IS NO FUN!


At the beginning of each round the server may make ONE proposition
* The proposition can add/remove/change the current game rules
* The proposition can NOT add/remove/change any of the golden rules
* A vote occurs, and if successful the new rule is in effect until the game is over or until someone votes it out


*******************
GAME RULES
These Rules may be changed only by propositions


You score points by getting to the server square (square 4)
* Serve with a nice hand toss to another player, above their waist
* Every round a you stay in square four, you get a point
* The first player to get 21 points wins (You keep your points when you go out)


You are out if your pass is declared TOO LOW or a SPIKE:
* Passes must be at least knee height of the player being passed to when the bag reaches the height of its arc
* Passes must go up before they go down


If you contact someone in their square, they may call interference on you.
* Players must vote on whether you interfered with or not


If the bag touches you on your arm, below your elbow, you are OUT
