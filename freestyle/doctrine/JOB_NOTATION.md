# JOB Notation Form

Governs the form of the semantic JOB-notation field (`freestyle_tricks.notation`):
the concise human-facing label that names a trick's structure. This field is
distinct from, and never a substitute for, the surfaces it sits beside:

- `operational_notation` — the bracketed execution notation whose token count
  equals the published ADD. Source priority and operational truth for that field
  live in `AUTHORITY.md`.
- `adds` — the numeric ADD accounting.
- the alias set — alternate and historical names.
- the public `description` — instructional prose.

This file governs only which *form* the JOB label takes. It does not rule identity,
ADD, or movement anatomy; those stay in `RED_RULINGS.md` and the doctrine queue.

`JOB_NOTATION.md` supersedes the former `NOTATION_STYLE_GUIDE` §6.5 as the canonical
authority for semantic JOB-label form.

## Form categories

**Decomposition form** — the component operators and base written left to right,
uppercased (`PARADOX WHIRL`, `PIXIE MIRAGE`, `STEPPING DUCKING PARADOX TORQUE`) — is
the default. Use it when the canonical identity is losslessly and unambiguously
decomposable under settled doctrine: every component is a registered operator or a
core atom, and the composition reproduces the trick with no residue.

**Canonical-name form** — the trick's own name uppercased — is used for:

- base tricks and core atoms (`MIRAGE`, `WHIRL`, `BUTTERFLY`, `OSIS`, `LEGOVER`,
  `TORQUE`, `DRIFTER`, `SWIRL`, `CLIPPER`, `ILLUSION`): the trick is its own
  structural identity, and lineage lives in family ancestry, not in a label that
  would restate the name;
- contested, lossy, or misleading decompositions;
- the individually documented exceptions in the registry below.

**Surface-delay form** — `{SURFACE} STALL` (`TOE STALL`, `HEAD STALL`) — is its own
governed category: the surface plus an explicit stall suffix, for beginner
readability.

**Direct-convention abbreviation** — a culturally universal short form for a base
primitive (`ATW` for around-the-world) — is kept by direct convention, and is not a
decomposition exception.

Verbosity alone is never a reason for name form. When a trick's decomposition is
unresolved or contested, its JOB label takes a **provisional name-form hold** —
revisited and moved to decomposition form when its named doctrine question closes.
A provisional hold is not a permanent exception.

## Authority and derivation

A JOB form is grounded, in priority order, by:

1. **Direct attestation** — a curator ruling or an outside source that names the
   form. An outside technical name (for example a FootbagMoves technical name the
   editorial decomposition agrees with) corroborates a decomposition form; it does
   not override the source-priority rules in `AUTHORITY.md` or a settled canonical
   identity.
2. **Mechanical derivation** — the form follows necessarily from the settled
   canonical identity, the registered operators, and the core atoms, with the ADD
   accounting and the operational notation agreeing.

A JOB value is never justified by its own prior existence. A row whose only support
is "this is what the field already held" is ungrounded and is treated as an open
item, not an authority.

## Core exemplars

Each exemplar is the current canonical value of a live, active row.

| Form | Exemplars | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Surface delay | `TOE STALL`, `HEAD STALL` | surface + explicit `STALL` suffix |
| Base / core atom | `MIRAGE`, `WHIRL`, `BUTTERFLY`, `OSIS`, `TORQUE`, `LEGOVER`, `DRIFTER`, `SWIRL`, `CLIPPER`, `ILLUSION` | name form; the trick is its own atom |
| Single-modifier compound | `PARADOX WHIRL`, `SPINNING WHIRL`, `PIXIE MIRAGE` (smear) | modifier + base, decomposition form |
| Multi-modifier compound | `DUCKING PARADOX SYMPOSIUM WHIRL` (mullet), `STEPPING DUCKING PARADOX TORQUE` (gauntlet) | stacked modifiers + base; decomposition form is kept even when a single name exists, because the components are clean and settled |
| Community abbreviation | `ATW` (around-the-world) | a culturally universal short form for a base primitive, kept by direct convention |

## Name-form exception registry

Every decomposable identity that nonetheless carries name form is listed here with
its authority class. An unlisted decomposable row takes decomposition form; a
name-form value is never justified by historical inconsistency alone. A provisional
hold is revisited when its named doctrine question closes.

| Row | JOB value | Authority class | Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| `mobius` | `MOBIUS` | direct convention | established community name; the gyro-torque lineage is preserved in the modifier links |
| `sumo` | `SUMO` | provisional hold | its Nuclear-Mirage-with-added-cross-dex decomposition is contested; revisits to decomposition form when the cross-dex reading settles |
| `barfly` | `BARFLY` | provisional hold | its Double-Infinity ontology is an open doctrine-queue question; revisits when that settles |

## Relationship to other doctrine

- `AUTHORITY.md` governs source priority and operational-notation truth; this file
  governs only the JOB label's form and defers to it on everything else.
- Identity merges, operator definitions, and movement-anatomy questions stay in
  `RED_RULINGS.md` and the doctrine queue. When such a question is open for a row,
  its JOB label takes the provisional name-form hold above until the question
  settles, rather than freezing a contested decomposition into the label.
