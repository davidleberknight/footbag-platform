# Freestyle research

Durable freestyle research, relocated out of the retiring `exploration/` scratch tree.

This is a research and evidence layer. It records analysis, theory, and methodology; it
is NOT ratified doctrine. Ratified rulings live in `freestyle/doctrine/`, and production
truth lives in `freestyle/inputs/` and the database. Nothing here should be read as
settled unless this index says so, and provisional or falsified findings must not be
promoted into doctrine or production data on the strength of living in a permanent
directory.

## Collections

### trick-universe-matrix (foundational movement-algebra theory)

- Establishes: the Phase-I movement-algebra framing (Slices 0 through 7) that the Atlas,
  the grammar work, and the Phase II-A enumeration all build on and cite.
- Status: foundational, treated as the frozen base the later work extends.
- Methods: analysis series; no generator.
- Open: none tracked; it is superseded only by a revision of the base framing.
- Informs: the Atlas volumes and the grammar-closure / Phase II-A research chain.

### movement-atlas (the Freestyle Movement Atlas, Volumes I through IV)

- Establishes (settled): the zero and one-dex universe (Volume I), a
  notation-as-representation study (Volume III), and a structural taxonomy (Volume IV).
  `atlas_catalog.csv` holds the one-dex coordinate catalog.
- Falsified: Volume II's two-dex six-cube was falsified as an upper bound only (see
  `volume-2/VOLUME_II_FALSIFICATION.md`). Read it as a bounded result, not a complete
  enumeration.
- Methods: `generate_atlas.py` and the per-volume generators regenerate the catalogs
  from dictionary notation; re-runnable and read-only.
- Open / restart: paused after Volume IV. `ATLAS_INDEX.md` carries the restart
  conditions and the unresolved-questions list.
- Informs: the same-side/opposite versus near/far distinction used in the
  positional-identity and Down-grid doctrine work; the doctrine-harvest bins in
  `DOCTRINE_HARVEST_AND_GLOSSARY_PROPOSAL.md` that the glossary-integration plan
  operationalizes; `atlas_catalog.csv` is read by the `movement-neighbor-v1` research
  (still in `exploration/`).
