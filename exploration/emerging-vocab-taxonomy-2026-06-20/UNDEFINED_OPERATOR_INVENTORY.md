## Reading

- `zulu` is the single highest-leverage definition. The head (~12 tokens) covers the bulk; the long
  tail is one-off folk names.
- Defining a token (operator status + ADD + chassis) lets its rows leave notation-blocked, the same
  way Pogo / Terraging / Blurry did. This list is the source-of-truth for that backlog.
- Notation abbreviations (dex / xbd / bs / dod), source typos, and now-settled operators (rake,
  neutron) are excluded: they are handled in the service reclassification, not operators to define.
- `dragon` is identification-deferred (Red), not a folk operator awaiting definition; flagged here
  because it still parses as an unknown token.
