## How to use the worksheet

For each entry write a ruling after `-> Red:`. The naming model is one true name
plus descriptive sub-names as aliases:

- **alias of <trick>** — folk name over a structure we already carry.
- **new canonical** — genuinely new structure; we derive notation + ADD.
- **historical variant of <trick>** — a name you recognize from the era; map, do
  not delete.
- **not a trick** — notation, concept, or header.

## The three groups (in the worksheet)
