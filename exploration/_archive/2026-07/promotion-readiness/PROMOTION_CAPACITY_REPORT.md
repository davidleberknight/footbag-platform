Read-only. Realistic estimate of the promotion frontier after the sprint's notation / grammar / operator / alias / topology / composition work, correcting the prior readiness audit which treated the 705 folk/unresolved as an undifferentiated authoring backlog.

## The correction
The prior audit said 36.7% ready and called the 705 folk structures a "691 decomposition-authoring campaign." Re-auditing by **recoverability from the name** (not by stored decomposition) shows **283 of the 705 are not authoring-blocked**: 172 are derivable from ratified grammar now, 111 are one operator ruling away. The true authoring backlog is ~406, not 691.

## How many of the 705 are actually recoverable now?
- **172 - immediately** (grammar-derivable from the name; just derive the notation, the step Wave 1 used).
- **111 - one operator ruling each** (clustered: zulu 29, symple 14, alpine 14, frantic 11, slapping 11, neutron 8, fusing 7, phasing 6, slaying 5, sonic 5, ...).
