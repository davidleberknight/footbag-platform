# Body-Topology Audit — Body-Action Ecosystem Ranking

**The shape of what the body does during tricks, the third topology study** (after how tricks end and how they begin). Counted by curated modifier membership, not formula token: the spin token alone appears in 177 tricks because rotational BASES (osis, torque, …) carry a spin in their own formula, so notation over-counts; the spinning ecosystem proper is 78 tricks. Body identity is classified by semantic role, since the entry audit established that operator position does not determine topological role.

**Three body axes dominate and are close in size:** Body Rotation (117), Body-Passage (108), and Dexterity-Operator (68). The single largest body ecosystem is **ducking (84)**, edging spinning (78); body-passage as a whole nearly equals body-rotation. The Airborne/Elevation axis has no curated ecosystem (see below).
