# Entry-Topology Audit — Entry-Identity Ranking (set-role model)

**The shape of how documented tricks are SET, mirror of the terminal ranking.** Toe-set (207) and clip-set (197) dominate as the grandparent entry roots. Once those two broad roots are set aside, **symposium, paradox, pixie, fairy, and stepping** emerge naturally as the largest entry identities in the documented vocabulary, the set-timing and set-ecosystem systems.

Entry identity is defined by **set-role**, not token location (see `ENTRY_METHODOLOGY.md`): the *what kind of set* a trick is. Body modifiers (spinning, ducking, gyro, diving) and operator overlays (whirling, swirling, miraging, barraging, tapping) act during flight and are held for a separate body-topology audit. Counts = distinct active tricks (651 active; 473 carry a formula).
