## The invisible set, decomposed
- 167 of 178 still appear in the family recursive ranking (have `trick_family`).
- 52 still appear in an entry ecosystem; 74 in a body ecosystem; 30 in a timing system (membership).
- **11** are FULLY invisible (no family, no membership, no notation): the only tricks absent from every audit.
- **143 of 178 are LOW difficulty** (their base_trick already has notation), so the compound formula is derivable, not authored from scratch.

## Bottom line
The family, entry-ecosystem, and body-ecosystem RANKINGS are robust today. What the missing 27.3% costs is the **component ecology** (under-counted across the board) and the **absolute surface / dex / catch counts**. Coverage work should target component + surface precision, not the rankings.
