### 2. Fairy-family

You've already confirmed Fairy as legitimate vocabulary — that part is
settled. Two things are still open:

**2a — Conceptual.** In modern understanding, is Fairy primarily:
(a) a set
(b) a dexterity concept

(e.g. Fairy Whirl, Fairy Butterfly), what does it add? FootbagMoves
records `Fairy ss X = 4` for several different X without a consistent
additive formula, which is why we've left this open.
