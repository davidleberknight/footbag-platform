# Freestyle Doctrine Audit — Post-Red Consolidation (2026-06-20)

Durable record of freestyle doctrine status after consolidating Red Husted's full pt1–pt14+
correspondence (Apr–Jun 2026). Supersedes the open-question framing of
`exploration/doctrine-audit-2026-06-19/DECISION_PACKET.md`, which predates this consolidation
and treats several since-answered items as open.

**Headline:** the frontier is no longer doctrine-bound. Of the packet's five "open doctrine"
questions, four are settled and one (Weaving) is genuinely open. The bottleneck is now execution
(authoring/verification), not Red consultation.

**Framing conventions (per curator, 2026-06-20):**
- `miraging` / `illusioning` / `whirling` / `swirling` are **operators**; `Mirage` / `Illusion` /
  `Whirl` / `Swirl` are **terminating family-parent tricks**. The same operator can occur uptime or
  downtime; an operator-vs-family description is not a contradiction.
- A "downtime set" is a category error. Red's later operator/family descriptions (e.g. "Eggbeater
  is a downtime illusioning legover") describe structure, not a literal timing-set doctrine, and do
  not supersede the set-level rulings.
