## 2. No-formula tricks

## 3. Counting-basis split (methodology note, not an error)

Formula atoms are occurrence-counted; ecosystem components are per-trick membership. The two bases are not directly comparable, and the table marks each row's basis. A spin token (206) over-counts vs the spinning ecosystem because rotational bases carry a base spin (same conflation as the body audit).

## 4. Named family components are compositions, not atoms

mirage-dex / osis / whirl etc. cannot be counted atomically without `trick_family` or an invariant map; they are recurring atom-combinations, reported in the comparison section.
