## THE single most important finding

**gyroMoves.txt resolves a 4-audit cross-cutting Wave 2 question via direct source evidence.**

The source operational notation uses `(back) SPIN [BOD]` for BOTH "Spinning X" (paradoxMoves precedent) AND "Gyro X" (gyroMoves) compounds. The two compound categories share an identical operational token; the source distinguishes only direction (back vs front), not turn-magnitude (full vs half).

The modern doctrine claim that "Spinning is full turn (360°). Gyro is half turn (180°)" (per `symbolicModifierEducation.ts:150`) has NO source-corpus operational precedent in 23 source compounds across 2 source documents (9 from gyroMoves + 14 from paradoxMoves).

This is the LEAD WAVE 2 QUESTION across the 4-audit corpus (FM FRP-5, Job RP-6, Paradox W2-15, Gyro GW2-1). Red Wave 2 adjudication is needed.
