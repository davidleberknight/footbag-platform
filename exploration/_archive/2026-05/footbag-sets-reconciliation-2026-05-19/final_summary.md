## THE three single most important findings

1. **5-audit Wave 2 LEAD question gains NEW resolution evidence.** Source distinguishes spinning vs gyro by ANCHOR + CONTINUATION (structural), not direction (back/front) or magnitude (full/half). Spinning: SET > (BACK) SPIN [BOD] > (any continuation). Gyro: CLIP > (BACK) SPIN [BOD] > (same dex component). Third axis to consider for Red Wave 2 adjudication.

2. **4-primitive dual identity matrix confirmed.** User's 2026-05-19 dual-identity clarification (originally for pixie) extends to a 4-primitive direction-quadrant matrix: pixie (same-in), fairy (same-out), quantum (op-in), atomic (op-out). All 4 are TOE-anchored 2-ADD tricks with single dex segment. All 4 are dual-identity (set modifier AND canonical trick row).

3. **2003 foundational set doctrine** verbatim documents sets as "uptime work" — "tricks on the way up" / "prefixes that can be put in front of trick names." This is the historical-precedent source for the modern Movement System Set/Uptime axis.
