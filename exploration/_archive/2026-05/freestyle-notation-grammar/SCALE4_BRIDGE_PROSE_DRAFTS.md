## 1. dimwalk — 4 ADD, pixie + butterfly

**Rationale.** Pixie's same-inside set on the butterfly base. The butterfly
4-ADD cohort (atomic-butterfly, ducking-butterfly, ripwalk, dimwalk) becomes
fully bridge-referenced once dimwalk is pilot — every butterfly compound at
this tier has a prose entry, and dimwalk's prereqs section anchors pixie's
butterfly appearance.

**short_description**
Pixie's same-inside set applied on the butterfly base — the bag passes behind the planted leg before the canonical butterfly wing opens on the second kick.

**execution_summary**
From a toe set, the first kick strikes same-side inside (pixie), sending the bag behind the supporting leg. The body opens for an opposite-side outside dex in the canonical butterfly wing motion, then closes with an opposite-side clipper cross-body delay. Pixie's signature is the same-inside opener; the rest of the trick rides on the butterfly's standard outside-wing pattern.

**learning_notes**
The same-inside first kick is closer to the body than canonical butterfly's outside-wing instinct. Practitioners coming from clean butterfly tend to anticipate the wing on the first kick, over-rotating into a butterfly opener. Keep the first kick tight; the wing belongs to the second kick.

**prerequisite_notes**
Butterfly (3 ADD) is the base. The same pixie mechanic appears across families — smoke (4 ADD, pixie + drifter), smear (3 ADD, pixie + mirage), paste (3 ADD, pixie + pickup), pigbeater (4 ADD, pixie + eggbeater) — practicing one carries pixie's same-inside feel to the others. Phoenix (5 ADD, pixie + ducking + butterfly) is the next-tier compound layering ducking onto this row.

## 2. smoke — 4 ADD, pixie + drifter

**Rationale.** Pixie's appearance on the drifter (a miraging-clipper rotational
stall). Smoke is the drifter-family entry into the pixie bridge — taking
pixie's same-inside set, learned on a non-rotational base like dimwalk, and
applying it on a rotational-stall frame.

**short_description**
On the drifter's miraging clipper foundation, the pixie set opens with a same-side inside dex — the bag passes behind the planted leg before the rotational landing.

**execution_summary**
The opening kick lands same-side inside (pixie), sending the bag behind the supporting leg. The drifter base carries the body into a miraging rotation toward the clipper-stall recovery; the bag lands on the clipper as the rotation completes. The pixie set is the only addition over canonical drifter — the rest is the drifter's miraging clipper pattern intact.

**learning_notes**
Pixie's same-inside set wants to be neat, but the drifter's miraging rotation kicks in fast; practitioners learning smoke after clean drifter tend to rush past the pixie opener into the rotation. Pause on the pixie set; let the bag clear the planted leg before the miraging body begins.

**prerequisite_notes**
Drifter (3 ADD) carries the miraging clipper-stall mechanic. The same pixie set appears across families — dimwalk (4 ADD, pixie + butterfly) and phoenix (5 ADD, pixie + ducking + butterfly) are the butterfly-base appearances; smoke is the drifter-base entry. Practicing dimwalk first gives the pixie feel on a non-rotational base before adding drifter's rotation.
