# Wave-2 Tier-1 Family-Ladder Impact

Date: 2026-05-11. Sourced from live `freestyle_tricks` query on 2026-05-11.

Sibling artifacts: `WAVE2_IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md`, `WAVE2_INSERTION_MATRIX.csv`.

Family-ladder rendering is the primary lateral-discovery surface on `/freestyle/tricks/:slug`. Wave-2 Tier-1 lands 6 rows across 4 families. This document captures the before/after for each affected family + the pedagogical pairings created by the new tier density.

## 1. butterfly family (+3 rows; 9 -> 12)

### Before

| ADD | Members |
|----:|---------|
| 3 | butterfly |
| 4 | atomic-butterfly, dimwalk, ducking-butterfly, parkwalk, ripwalk, sidewalk, spinning-butterfly |
| 5 | bigwalk |

### After Wave-2

| ADD | Members |
|----:|---------|
| 3 | butterfly |
| 4 | atomic-butterfly, dimwalk, ducking-butterfly, parkwalk, ripwalk, sidewalk, spinning-butterfly, **tripwalk** |
| 5 | bigwalk, **matador**, **phoenix** |

### Pedagogical impact

- **5-ADD tier triples** (1 -> 3 members). bigwalk previously stood alone as the 5-ADD butterfly representative; matador adds a clean 2-modifier compound (nuclear+butterfly) and phoenix adds a 3-modifier compound (pixie+ducking+butterfly). Learners moving up from 4-ADD have a denser progression band.
- **4-ADD tier modifier diversity:** atomic / dimwalk / ducking / parkwalk / ripwalk / sidewalk / spinning + **quantum** (tripwalk). Quantum is the first explicit pt10-settled quantum-modifier butterfly compound in the family. Stronger educational stop for the quantum modifier as a token.
- **Cross-row notation comparisons** at 5 ADD: bigwalk (canonical decomp tbd) vs matador (nuclear+butterfly) vs phoenix (pixie+ducking+butterfly) creates a 3-way 5-ADD butterfly trio that exercises three different compositional patterns reaching the same difficulty value. Strong teaching material.

### After Wave-2

| ADD | Members |
|----:|---------|
| 5 | paradox-blender |
| 6 | food-processor, spender, **mind-bender** |

### Pedagogical impact

- **6-ADD tier becomes a 3-way comparison** (food-processor vs spender vs mind-bender).
- **Direct A/B teaching pair:** spender (spinning + paradox + blender) vs mind-bender (ducking + paradox + blender). Same base, same +paradox modifier, differs only on spinning-vs-ducking. This is the cleanest "modifier-substitution" pedagogical exhibit currently available in the dictionary. Spinning carries body-spin difficulty; ducking carries head-low compression. Same paradox-blender frame, two different body modifiers.

The Mind Bender flagship page (per UX2 pilot considerations) gains particular value from this comparison; a future trick-detail page can hard-link to spender's page as the obvious "Related" sibling.
