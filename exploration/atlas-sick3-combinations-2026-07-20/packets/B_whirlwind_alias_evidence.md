## Recommended answer

Scope the alias by source era rather than picking a single winner: keep `whirlwind ->
sole_survivor` as the live search alias (it is already curator-ruled), and record the
footbag.org-era sense (Spinning Paradox Symposium Whirl, 6 ADD) in the divergence
registry per the record-don't-adopt policy, so record resolution can cite whichever sense
the source era supports. For these two records specifically: the 2002/2003 era predates
the PassBack folk-name lineage, and footbag.org was the era's naming authority, so if a
single reading must be chosen for the records, the 6-ADD footbag.org sense is the better
historical fit. Curator decides; both records stay uncited until then.
