## 1. THE foundational doctrinal finding

**Source establishes flag-count-equals-ADD as THE canonical ADD math doctrine** (verbatim, source line 4):

> "Each of the following is what makes up the technical difficulty of a freestyle move. Each of these is equal to one Add (element of difficulty)."

Followed by definitions of the 5 ADD categories:

1. **DELAY [del]** — catch (stall/delay) the footbag on foot or part of leg
2. **DEXTERITY [dex]** — circle or cross the footbag with leg
3. **UNUSUAL SURFACE [uns]** — any surface besides toes, insteps, outsteps, knees
4. **BODY [bod]** — spin, jump, or twist of body
5. **CROSS BODY [xbd]** — kick or delay on opposite side of body

**This is THE foundational doctrine for all ADD math.** Modern operational notation flag vocabulary is a 23-year-continuous descendant of this 5-category source.

The implication: **flag-count = ADD count**. Each bracketed flag in operational notation = 1 ADD. Total ADD = total flag count.

Modifier-stack math (e.g., paradox(+1) + mirage(2) = 3) is a PEDAGOGICAL derivation that should mathematically equal the flag count when correctly modeled. When the two diverge, **the flag count is canonical per this source**.

## 3. CRITICAL RESOLUTION CANDIDATE: Pogo 5-audit cross-cutting question

The 4-audit pogo/symposium (no plant while) ambiguity question (FM FRP-7, Job RP-7, paradoxMoves PD-2/W2-3, footbag-sets SW2-5) has been a HIGH-priority Wave 2 issue. This source provides direct resolution evidence:

**Source pogo definition** (verbatim, source line 47-52):
> "'Pogo' is a concept where the set is from a cross body delay but is flicked immediately under the support leg. Basically this is a concept that refers to a Clipper set Symposium Mirage done in front of another move. (Thereby adding a dexterity to the front of the move, just as with Pixie, but a little more difficult to execute because you don't put your foot down and you get the symposium body add. **Therefore a pogo-style move is usually worth 2 adds more than its non-pogo namesake.**)"

**Modern doctrine:** pogo=+0/+0 set modifier (Red pt5-locked).

**Apparent contradiction:** Source says pogo adds +2; modern says pogo adds +0.

**RESOLUTION:** The two doctrines are reconcilable under the flag-count framework:
- Source's "+2 more" = STRUCTURAL contribution counted as operational flags: pogo adds 1 [DEX] flag (the pre-dex segment) + 1 [BOD] flag (the symposium body element).
- Modern's "+0" = INCREMENTAL modifier value above what's already in the operational flag count.

Both are correct in their own counting frames. The 4-audit pogo-compound systematic +1 ADD divergence (paradoxMoves PD-2: 5 compounds with source 5-ADD vs modern 4-ADD derivation) is explained: source counts the +1 dex from pogo's pre-segment + the +1 body from symposium = +2. Modern multiplier doctrine treats pogo modifier as +0 because the +2 is already counted via the operational notation flags. Both produce the canonical flag-count-equals-ADD result.

This is the lead RESOLUTION CANDIDATE for the 4-audit cross-cutting Wave 2 question. Glossary integration should explain this reconciliation explicitly.
