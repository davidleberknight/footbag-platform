# Insight Registry — The Single Authority

**This file is authoritative for the Glossary V2 insight list.** It supersedes the
candidate list sketched in `ARCHITECTURE.md §3` and the Layer-9 sinks in
`DEPENDENCY_GRAPH.md`; where they differ, this file wins. There is exactly one insight
list, and it is here.

## Governing rules

- **An entry may carry a Reveal only for an insight in this table.** A proposed Reveal
  whose idea is not registered (and does not earn admission here) is a Relates line in
  disguise. This is the mechanical guard against Reveal sprawl — the exact failure the
  pilot caught.
- **Each insight has one canonical home** (the entry or essay that states it in full).
  The one exception is the algebra insight, which has three sequenced home-entries (a
  staircase). Every other entry that touches an insight carries a **one-line reference
  in its Relates layer**, never a restatement.
- **Additions and rewordings are curator-governed** (James), like a canonical doc. The
  registry is meant to stay small; growth is the smell of Reveal inflation.
- **Draft status.** Homes marked *(flagship)* are validated by written pilot entries;
  homes marked *(planned)* are not yet authored. Insight wording is provisional until
  James signs off (the voice/insight owner ruling).

## Notes on shape

- **Eleven insights, not twelve or "about a dozen."** The collapse of the three algebra
  facets into one (row 5) is the whole reason this registry exists; do not re-split them.
- **Insight homes cluster in the flagship + tranche-2 set.** Rows 1, 3, 5, 6, 7 are
  fully home-validated by written pilot entries. Rows 2, 4, 8 need Core-Concept entries
  (near-term, tranche 2 territory). Rows 9, 10, 11 have planned homes further out; row 10
  is explicitly V1.2 because notation sits late in the dependency-graph order.
- **Foundational Physical Skills are not on this list.** Being a learner gateway (Toe
  Stall, Clipper Stall, ATW, Mirage, Butterfly, Osis) is a separate property from being
  an insight home. Some Skills are homes (Toe Stall, Mirage, Butterfly); some are not
  (ATW, Osis, Clipper... — Clipper shares row 3). Do not conflate the two lists.
- **Mirror Law is an essay, not a registry row.** It is the public face of insights 1
  and 2 (and illustrated by 6). The registry holds the insights; the essay assembles
  them.

## What this file is not

Not the entry list, not the section spine, not the Foundational Physical Skills list
(that is ruled in ARCHITECTURE.md §6 and RULINGS_GATING_QUESTIONS.md). It is only the
authoritative catalog of genuine insights and where each lives.
