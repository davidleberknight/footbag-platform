## 1. Central finding (must surface FIRST in any glossary integration)

**gyroMoves.txt resolves a 4-audit cross-cutting Wave 2 question via direct source evidence.**

The source operational notation uses `(back) SPIN [BOD]` for BOTH "Spinning X" compounds (precedent in paradoxMoves) AND "Gyro X" compounds (this audit's source). The two compound categories share an identical operational token.

The modern doctrine claim that "Spinning is full turn (360°). Gyro is half turn (180°)" (per `symbolicModifierEducation.ts:150`) has NO source-corpus operational precedent. The source distinguishes only direction: back-spin vs front-spin.

Three interpretations the curator + Red Wave 2 must adjudicate:
1. **Modern doctrine has drifted from 2003-source.** Spinning and gyro were originally the same operator (back-spin); the full-vs-half distinction was introduced post-source.
2. **Modern doctrine adds magnitude implicitly to operational tokens.** Operational `(back) SPIN [BOD]` is contextually full-turn or half-turn depending on the compound's structural role. If formalized, the operational grammar §13 would need a magnitude token.
3. **Spinning and gyro are equivalent operators with semantic shading.** Both are +1 back-spin; the "full vs half" framing is pedagogical (a coach hint) rather than structural.

This is the LEAD WAVE 2 QUESTION across the 4-audit corpus (FM FRP-5, Job RP-6, Paradox W2-15, Gyro GW2-1).

## 2. Major findings worth surfacing

1. **Vortex is canonically gyrating drifter, NOT paradox.** Three-source alignment: paradox-tutorial.txt 1999 (explicit example), paradoxMoves (excludes vortex), gyroMoves (canonical "Vortex (Gyro Drifter)"). This is THE textbook example of "spinning subsumes paradox" doctrine.
2. **Inspinning is structurally distinct from spinning.** Source has 3 explicit Inspinning Same Side compounds using `(front) SPIN [BOD]`. Modern doctrine has not registered "inspinning" as a modifier — this is a recurring gap (also surfaced by paradoxMoves W2-15 + FM FRP-5 + Job RP-6).
3. **The "Same Side" naming convention has 3 explicit precedents** in this source (Inspinning Same Side Illusion / Mirage / Butterfly). The qualifier indicates the TOE-start operational variant where the dex is on the SAME leg as the spin.
4. **Mobius carries 3 modern chain readings** in the symbolic equivalences registry: 'gyro torque' / 'spinning ss torque' / 'spinning ss miraging op osis'. The presence of "spinning ss" as an alternate reading reinforces the spinning/gyro operational-equivalence question (GW2-3).
5. **Big Apple is a worked example for the gyro(+1) + symposium(+1) + torque(4) = 6 ADD multiplier-stack.** Strong source precedent for multiplier doctrine's class-C structural multiplier classification.
6. **Source provides dual operational variants for 7 of 9 compounds** (CLIP-start AND TOE-start). The dual-form convention is pedagogical: shows structural-equivalence at different starting positions.
7. **The OP BACK SWIRL [DEX] compound token** appears in Spyro Gyro (1 of 9) — 4th source occurrence in the 4-audit corpus. Confirms it as a recurring source convention.
