34 rows, unlocked mechanically by the distributive same-side

Structural cause: the observational universe generator derives its ladder
state from the older reconciliation sources, not from the ruling ledger, so
every July resolution (alias collapses, operator-gate moves, mirror
deferrals, the positional session) is invisible to the public queue. 39 queue
rows are not ledger rows at all, and 22 more sit in a different ledger state
than the page shows.

## 2. Nuclear case study

- **Bare Nuclear is a set/operator, not a trick.** Its formula is open-ended
  by design (no terminal contact), the trick-termination rule excludes
  modifier-only sequences, and the ledger already carries it as
  not-a-trick-object. Disposition B: remove from the trick-authoring queue;
  the Set Encyclopedia is its home.
- **The doctrine is settled, not under review**: the registry carries nuclear
  as a +2 set modifier decomposing to paradox plus a downtime illusioning
  dex, and twelve plain nuclear compounds are LIVE canonicals (whirl,
  illusion, legover, pickup, guay, drifter, double leg over, dyno, torque,
  rev whirl, rev guay, butterfly), every one opening
  `CLIP > SAME OUT [PDX] [DEX] >`. The set surface's "Under review" badge is
  stale.
- **The prose/notation mismatch is real but small**: the operator reference's
  one-line meaning says "Operational form: CLIP > SAME OUT >" without the
  `[PDX]` token that every shipped compound carries. The paradox hip shift IS
  in the formal notation (as `[PDX]` on the lead dex); only the display
  string omits it. One-line prose fix.
- **Compound consequences**: Nuclear ss Butterfly, ss Dyno, ss Whirl, far
  Butterfly, far Mirage, near Butterfly, and Nuclear Nova are ALREADY
  curated aliases (barfry, nuclear_dyno, hurl, matador, sumo, barfry,
  oh_wheely): bucket C, and their queue presence is generator staleness. The
  seven remaining ss forms (guay, illusion, legover, mirage, pickup, reverse
  guay, reverse whirl) compose a settled +2 prefix with live plain-form
  canonicals under the ratified distributive same-side rule: bucket A.
  Nuclear Ducking Mirage (folk name Sumo Thong, distinct from sumo = the far
  form) composes three settled components: bucket A.
