		Blurry Moves	 
		
Blizzard
(Stepping Paradox Illusion)
4 adds	 	
CLIP > OP IN [DEX] > OP OUT [PDX] [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
Identical to the Blur delay except the second mirage dexterity is in the other direction Eg. From a right clipper delay- quickly bring the left leg over the bag from in to out. As you put the left leg down hop off the right leg and bring it around the bag from out to in. Now hop off the leg left and catch the bag on a left toe delay.

Blur*
(Stepping Paradox Mirage)
4 adds	 	
CLIP > OP IN [DEX] > OP IN [PDX] [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
A mirage into a paradox mirage. Eg. From a right clipper bring the left leg over the footbag to the ground simultaneously bringing the right leg around the footbag from in to out- just before planting the right leg hop off the left leg and catch the footbag on a left toe delay.

Symposium
Blur
5 adds	 	
CLIP > OP IN [DEX] > (NO PLANT WHILE) OP IN/OUT [DEX] [BOD] [BOD] > OP TOE [DEL]
This is a blurry symposium paradox mirage. Eg. Set this move from a Right clipper delay. Set the bag across your body and do a left leg mirage. Now hop hard off your right leg and do a right leg symposium in to out mirage- catching the footbag in a left toe delay.
