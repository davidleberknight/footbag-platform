FREESTYLE MOVE LIST 

			
		Pixie Moves	 
		
Pixie
2 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
From a toe delay- circle the footbag from in to out and catch on the toe of the support leg.

 	[dex] [del]
Terrage
(Double Pixie)
3 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > SAME IN [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL].
A double pixie. Eg. Set with right toe. Circle the footbag from in to out twice landing on a left toe delay.

 	[dex] [dex] [del]
Smudge
(Pixie Illusion)
3 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > OP OUT [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
Basically the reverse dexterity version of Smear (pixie mirage). Eg. Set the footbag with your right toe- execute a right leg in to out dexterity- then a left leg out to in dexterity- then delay the footbag on your right toe.

 	[dex] [dex] [del]
Smear
(Pixie Mirage)
3 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > OP IN [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
Pixie the footbag straight to a mirage. E.g., from a right toe delay circle the footbag from in to out. Hop off the left leg and bring it around the footbag - from in to out - then hop off the right leg and toe delay the footbag on the setting leg.

 	[dex] [dex] [del]
Pixie Opposite Clipper
3 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > OP CLIP [XBD] [DEL]
From a left toe set, perform a quick in to out dexterity with the setting leg. End by catching the bag on a right foot clipper.

 	[dex] [xbd] [del]
Pixie Same Clipper
3 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > SAME CLIP [XBD] [DEL]
From a left toe set, perform a quick in to out dexterity with the setting leg. End by catching the bag on a left foot clipper.

Pixie Same Side Illusion
3 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] (plant) > SAME OUT [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
From a right toe, perform a quick in to out dexterity (pixie) and touch the ground with your right foot. Then, come back around the bag from out to in ending on a left toe delay.

 	[dex] [dex] [del]
Pixie
Leg Over
3 adds	 	
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > OP IN/OUT [DEX] > SAME TOE [DEL]
An around-the-world set (pixie) to the opposite leg-over. E.g., from a right toe set, quickly circle the footbag from in to out with your right foot; hop off the left leg passing this leg over the top of the bag and landing a left toe delay.

 	[dex] [dex] [del]
Symposium
Pixie
3 adds	 	
TOE (plant) > (no plant while) SAME IN [BOD] [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
Eg. Set from a right toe. Plant the right foot and raise the left foot. Hop off of the right leg bringing it up and over the bag from in to out catching the bag on a left toe delay. **The left leg does not touch the ground after the initial set.
