		Paradox Moves	 
		
Paradox
Illusion
3 adds	 	
CLIP > SAME OUT [PDX[DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
A Paradox Revese Mirage. From a clipper set, set the bag about waist high. Turn toward the bag and complete an out to in dexterity with the setting foot. Complete the move by catching the bag on your opposite toe.

Paradox
Mirage*
3 adds	 	
CLIP > SAME IN [PDX] [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
E.g., from a right clipper delay, set the bag so it's moving across your body from left to right, then bring your right leg around the left leg and around the footbag as it falls. You must in this case circle the footbag from the left of the footbag to the front and finally to the right ending on a left toe delay. Basically the paradox leg completes a tight sharp 'S' shape with the support leg in the first loop and the footbag in the second loop of the letter 'S'.

4 adds	 	
CLIP > SAME IN [PDX] [DEX] > OP IN [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
Eg. From a left foot clipper stall- bring the left leg around the support leg and around the bag from in to out completing the 'S' shape. Then hop off your right leg and bring it around the bag from in to out landing a left toe delay.

Paradox Double Leg Over
4 adds	 	
CLIP > SAME IN [PDX] [DEX] > OP OUT [DEX] > SAME TOE [DEL]
Eg. Set from a left foot clipper delay- do a left leg in to out dexterity- then a right leg out to in dexterity and catch the footbag on a right toe delay. This is a paradox mirage with an extra legover at the end.

Paradox Symposium Mirage
4 adds	 	
CLIP (plant) > (no plant while) SAME IN [PDX] [BOD] [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]
Eg. Set the footbag from your right foot clipper and switch your support leg (raise your left leg as you plant the right foot). Then, without planting the left foot, hop off of the right leg bringing it up and over the bag from in to out ending on a left toe delay.

Paradox Whirl
4 adds	 	
CLIP > SAME IN [PDX] [DEX] > OP CLIP [XBD] [DEL]
A whirl delay that is set from one clipper delay and caught on the other. Set the bag so it moves across the front of your body and bring the setting leg from behind the support leg- and without putting this leg down- begin the whirl motion and end on a clipper using the other leg to do the delay.

Video demonstration available!

4 adds

Identical to the Blur delay except the second mirage dexterity is in the other direction Eg. From a right clipper delay- quickly bring the left leg over the bag from in to out.
