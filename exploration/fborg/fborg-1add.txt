



FOOTBAG WORLDWIDE: FREESTYLE :1-ADD MOVES

FREESTYLE MOVE LIST 

			
		1-Add Moves	 
		
Around the World Kick
1 add	 	Circle the footbag in between toe kicks.	 	[dex]
Clipper Kick
1 add	 	Kick the footbag with the inside surface of the foot with the kicking leg tucked behind the support leg.	 	[xbd]
Cloud Kick
1 add	 	Kick the footbag with the calf of the leg.	 	[uns]
Flying Inside
1 add	 	Jump and kick the footbag with the inside surface of the foot.	 	[bod]
Flying Outside
1 add	 	Jump and kick the footbag with the outside surface of the foot.	 	[bod]
Forehead Delay
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the forehead.	 	[uns]
Frigidosis
1 add	 	Frigidosis is an inside delay caught behind your body, but not in the cross-body position. The best way to visualize it is to go for an osis, make the catch, but don't spin. So, you "freeze" the bag in a non-cross-body position.	 	[del]
Head Stall
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the top of the head. Look slightly upwards to the get the top of the head perfectly flat.	 	[uns]
Inside Delay*
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the inside surface of the foot.	 	[del]
Knee Delay
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the top of the thigh.	 	[del]
Neck Delay
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the back of the neck.	 	[uns]
Outside Delay*
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the outside surface of the foot.	 	[del]
Peak Delay
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the peak of your cap/hat. This can be dismounted by a flick of the head or 'pinging' your peak.	 	[uns]
Shoulder Delay
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the shoulder. Use your cheek at first to help to catch the footbag.	 	[uns]
Sole Kick
1 add	 	Kick the footbag with the sole of the foot.	 	[uns]
Spin
1 add	 	Spin around in between kicks/delays.	 	[bod]
Toe Delay*
1 add	 	Delay the footbag on the top of the shoe (above the toes).
Video demonstration available!
