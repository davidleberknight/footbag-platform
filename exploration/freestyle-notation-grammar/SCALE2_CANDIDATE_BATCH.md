# SCALE-2 Candidate Batch — whirl ecosystem focus

Second scalable enrichment wave. Where SCALE-1 lifted the 10 LOW-RISK
op-notation-seeded rows from the original Wave-1/2 seed, SCALE-2 takes the
whirl ecosystem — the largest single family in the dictionary at 17 rows — and
fills the prose tier for its base + single-modifier + selected two-modifier
compounds. **No ontology mutation. No new tricks. No new media ingestion.**
Pilot prose only.

## Why whirl

Whirl is the dictionary's largest family (17 rows) and the only family with a
record-holder demonstration at every density tier already on disk. After SCALE-1
two whirl rows are pilot (hatchet, montage); the remaining 15 form a clear
pedagogical ladder from the 3-ADD base through 7-ADD flagship. Enriching the
ladder converts whirl from "two pilot pages plus 15 sparse" into a fully
authored family tree.

Critically: whirl-family enrichment unblocks cross-family pedagogical pairings
already partially seeded by SCALE-1. Tap (mirage tapping, pilot) pairs with a
prose'd tapping-whirl; hatchet (whirl diving, pilot) pairs with a prose'd
ducking-whirl; mullet (pilot deferral) sits next to montage on the
modifier-layering ladder.

### Risk notes

- **blurry-whirl** — pt12 queue item #1: "Blurry Whirl canonical decomposition (math conflict under pt11 +1)". The trick's ADD math is unresolved. Authoring prose against a contested ADD would freeze the page on a value the ontology may shift away from. Wait for pt12 close.
- **rev-whirl, rev-up** — direction variants of whirl with zero modifier_links. The decomposition strip won't fire; the formula degenerates to identity (`rev-whirl = 3 ADD`). Pilot prose is technically possible but the educational value is much lower than for compound rows. Defer until the modifier-link pool is more saturated.
- **surreal** — three modifier links (paradox + spinning + stepping) means it activates the modifier-layering panel like Montage and Mullet. No ontology risk, but it has zero records and no op-notation. Hold for SCALE-3 once the 4 + 5 ADD whirl tier is settled.
- **tapping-whirl** — zero records, and tap (mirage tapping, already pilot from SCALE-1) covers the tapping modifier's feel cross-family. Defer to SCALE-3 to keep this batch at 10.

audit should still flag formulaic sentence-opening patterns and templated
