**short_description**: clean (no 2-word opener appears more than once).

**execution_summary** (4 patterns at ≥ 2x):
- `The body` — 5x (ducking-whirl, paradox-symposium-whirl, symposium-whirl, whirl ×2). - `The whirl` — 4x. Family base named as subject. **Acceptable**.
- `The supporting` — 2x. **Acceptable** at 2x.

**learning_notes**: `Hold the` 2x and `If the` 2x — both at floor threshold, no concern.

**prerequisite_notes**: `Whirl (3` 3x — three rows naming whirl (3 ADD) as their direct prereq. Structurally inherent; **acceptable**.
