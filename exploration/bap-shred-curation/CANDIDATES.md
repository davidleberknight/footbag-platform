# Individual Shred Videos — BAP candidate curation review

Source: bigaddposse.com single-page roster (84 invite-only BAP members, organized by
induction year 1992-2025). Each member carries one external representative video link
(YouTube / Vimeo / Dailymotion / MySpace) or none. These links are the candidate pool
for a new on-platform "Individual Shred Videos" folder.

## Triage legend
- CURATE: live individual-player shred video, no platform/duplication issue (subject to a subjective quality pass — see note).
- HOLD: needs a decision or a liveness/embeddability check before inclusion.
- REJECT: defunct platform or otherwise unusable.
- (no video): member has no representative video on the roster; not a candidate.

## Note on subjective criteria
Instructional value / historical significance / video quality / representation quality
require actually viewing each clip. Those are NOT assessed below (no viewing capability);
every CURATE here is CURATE-on-objective-signals and still needs a human/viewing pass.
